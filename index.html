About

I'm a UX Design Principal who works at the intersection of complex enterprise workflows and the people who depend on them to get their jobs done — procurement teams, quality analysts, and collections agents making high-stakes decisions under time pressure.

My approach starts with systems thinking: mapping how fragmented tools and data actually flow through a business before redesigning the experience around the roles that use them. I've led UX strategy end-to-end, from research and information architecture through the design systems that keep multi-product platforms cohesive as they scale.
